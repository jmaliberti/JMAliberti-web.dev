A Bit About Me

I live by the beach in Southern California, with my husband and our dog, Falkor. I've always been the tech-person of my household and still spend time at my parents' calibrating their surround-sound speakers and smart TV. It has always been my dream to have a job that allowed me to use my creative and puzzle-solving metality to fulfill my career potential. Being in the tech field just never occured to me despite how much I used and relied on it. At least, not until my life had changed quite suddenly.

My Background

I studied at UC Irvine and earned my B.A. in English with an Emphasis in Creative Writing. After college, my main focus was working to pay off my loans. I had jobs in sales, customer service and even managed a day care center for several years each. Then the pandemic happened. It was then that I turned to SkillCrush. Getting a job as a leasing administrator, I studied during my lunch breaks and weekends to become a Front-End Web Developer. Now, here I am ready to help you!
